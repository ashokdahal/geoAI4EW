Apart from Scientific Research, I am also involved in various projects aimed towards providing real-world solutions to natural hazard and risk related problems. The following are the select few and major projects that I have been involved.

The earthquake prevention system, and the earthquake prevention program:
Novel and patented method to predict earthquake location and magnitude using GNSS data.

Implementation of Integrated Geospatial Platform, Database, and Applicationsfor Disaster Risk Management in Uttarakhand, India:
Decision support system for monitoring natural hazards and emergency response.

Flood and Storm Surge Risk Assessment Tools for ADB Spatial Data AnalysisExplorer (SPADE):
Spatial decision support tools for Asian Development Bank to assess flood and storm surge risks on their infrastructure. 

RiskChanges- A Spatial Decision Support System for the Analysis of Changing Risk for Natural Hazards:
GIS based multi-hazard risk assessment system for understanding the risk and mitigation planning on multiple cost and climate change scenarios.
